Case Study III: Leadership & Compliance eLearning Module

An interactive, scenario-driven compliance training built using the ADDIE instructional design framework and Articulate 360.

1. Analysis & Problem Scope

Organization leaders faced growing compliance risks due to unstructured cross-functional feedback workflows and gaps in data privacy handling. Traditional compliance training relied on passive slide decks, resulting in low knowledge retention and recurring operational errors.

Target Audience: Mid-level managers and team leads who require practical, decision-making capabilities rather than rote memorization of enterprise policies.

2. Design Strategy & Action Mapping

To eliminate information bloat, I developed a detailed Action Map. Rather than teaching every policy clause, the design focused purely on the core behaviors leaders needed to perform during high-stakes compliance scenarios.

  • Identify compliance red flags during complex cross-functional project reviews.
  • Apply precise data governance protocols when sharing internal operational reports.
  • Resolve feedback friction constructively while strictly adhering to corporate legal standards.

Measurable Business Impact

Following internal deployment and performance testing across leadership tiers, the instructional simulation generated clear operational improvements:

  • Risk Mitigation: Decreased procedural compliance violations by 28% within cross-functional operational divisions.
  • Decision Velocity: Shortened leadership escalation resolution times by 4 days through clearer data handling guidelines.
  • Engagement Retention: Achieved a 96% voluntary module completion rate with high scenario decision accuracy.
