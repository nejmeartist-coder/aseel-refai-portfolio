Case Study III: Leadership & Compliance eLearning Module

An interactive, scenario-driven compliance training built using the ADDIE instructional design framework and Articulate 360.

1. Analysis (The Challenge & Audience)

The Business Need: Organization leaders faced growing compliance risks due to unstructured cross-functional feedback workflows and gaps in data privacy handling. Traditional compliance training relied on passive slide decks, resulting in low knowledge retention and recurring operational errors.

Target Audience: Mid-level managers and team leads who require practical, decision-making capabilities rather than rote memorization of policies.

2. Design (Instructional Strategy & Action Mapping)

To eliminate information bloat, I began by creating a detailed Action Map. Rather than teaching every policy clause, the design focused purely on the actions leaders needed to take during high-stakes compliance scenarios.

  • Identify compliance red flags during cross-functional reviews.
  • Apply correct data governance protocols when sharing reports.
  • Resolve feedback friction constructively while adhering to legal standards.

3. Development (Interactive eLearning Build)

Using Articulate 360, I developed a modern, branching-scenario module that places learners directly into realistic workplace dilemmas. Immediate formative feedback guides learners through the consequences of their choices, reinforcing compliance protocols safely.

4. Implementation & Evaluation

The module was deployed as a self-paced web package. Evaluation strategies include embedded knowledge checks, scenario-based branching assessments, and completion tracking aligned with corporate compliance standards.
