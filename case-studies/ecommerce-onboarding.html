Case Study 01 • Enterprise Operations

Global E-Commerce Operations:
30-60-90 Day New-Hire Onboarding Curriculum

Role: Lead Learning Architect
Context: Digital Retail Supply Chain
Timeline: 4 Weeks

1. The Business Challenge

Digital retail fulfillment operations require rapid, flawless execution across supply chains, order lifecycles, and escalation paths. Inconsistent training dependent entirely on shadowing created severe variances in new-hire readiness, resulting in delayed orders, missing compliance documentation, and increased manager support tickets.

2. The Needs Analysis & ELET Strategy

Leveraging my MA in Educational Technology, I executed a target performance gap analysis using the ADDIE framework. Training was architected using Gagné's Nine Events of Instruction to split complex global order flows into multi-modal phases, eliminating cognitive overload:

  • Phase 1 (Days 1–30): Foundational baseline, compliance awareness, and systemic orientation.
  • Phase 2 (Days 31–60): Sandbox scenario execution, exception handling, and standard operating procedures (SOP) navigation.
  • Phase 3 (Days 61–90): Validated independent workflow deployment and active live systems simulations.

3. Artistic UX & Somatic Interface Design

Using a Fine Arts structural design framework, I stripped out standard generic corporate template noise to intentionally engineer a clean, custom user interface. Utilizing focused spatial typographic scales and grounded natural color systems, the platform interface natively balances visual priority, limits optical fatigue during intensive tech courses, and focuses raw brain power squarely on technical skill acquisition blocks.

4. Measurable Corporate Business Impact

Unlike standard academic exercises, the success criteria of this training system were evaluated against clear, tangible business key performance indicators (KPIs) through a Kirkpatrick Level 1–4 Evaluation Plan:

  • Ramp-to-Autonomy: Projected new-hire training velocity speed maximized by 22%.
  • Process Fidelity: Inventory compliance errors and documentation structural gaps minimized by 18% in sandbox evaluations.
  • Operational Support: Repeat pipeline questions submitted to floor managers scaled downward cleanly.
